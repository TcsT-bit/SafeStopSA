3 Rules If Stopped

  1. Don’t stop in a dark area. Drive slowly with hazards on to a police station, garage, or busy place.
  2. Ask for ID. Real officers show SAPS ID and force number.
  3. Call 10111 while driving. Ask them to confirm if it’s real.
